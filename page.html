About

What We Do

The NYVCA is the only non-profit group in the greater New York region solely dedicated to supporting the Venture Capital and entrepreneurial communities.

Membership is by-invitation only and we offer our members access to deal flow, valuable customers, distribution channels, media and investment capital through its events and programming.


Programs & Events

The NYVCA hosts regularly scheduled events.

Leading Edge Dinner Series

Monthly, private dinners for VCs, emerging growth company founders, CEOs, CFOs and thematic-based dinners

Breakfast and Evening Receptions

Often combined with fireside chats or panel discussions

Custom Events

In conjunction with our members or corporate sponsors

Featured: Annual INGENUITY Conference


Sponsorship

We believe that sponsors provide a crucial role in educating and advising our members and their portfolio companies on key topics to accelerate growth.


Community

The NYVCA believes deeply in supporting and fostering the entrepreneurial community in New York City. We team up with public and private constituencies in support of promoting the broader ecosystem.


Research

The NYVCA also conducts research in conjunction with our corporate partners and disseminates findings to our members, Press and related partners. Stay tuned for results of our 10-year study on Venture Capital in NYC.
